India Sarkari Naukri.com: Latest Sarkari Exam Admit Card 2025

Good news for candidates preparing for government jobs in India! Now, easily download all government recruitment exam admit cards, hall tickets, and entrance letters from the India Sarkari Naukri website. Get your admit card today for over 5000+ government job exams scheduled in 2025.

Importance of Sarkari Admit Card

The admit card (Hall Ticket) is a mandatory document for appearing in government exams. It contains essential details such as the candidate’s name, roll number, exam date, exam center, and other instructions. Without the admit card, entry to the exam center is not allowed. Therefore, downloading and keeping it safe on time is crucial.

Admit Cards Available on India Sarkari Naukri.com

Our website provides admit cards for various government recruitments, including:

1. Banking Exams : SBI PO, IBPS Clerk, IBPS PO, RBI Assistant, etc.

2. Railway Recruitment : RRB NTPC, RRB Group D, RPF Constable, RRB JE, RPF, Technician etc.

3. Union Public Service Commission (UPSC): Civil Services Exam, NDA, CDS, etc.

4. State-Level Exams: UPPSC, BPSC, MPPSC, RPSC, RSSB, DSSSB, etc.

5. SSC Exams: SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, SSC MTS, SSC GD Constable, etc.

6. Education Sector Recruitment: CTET, UPTET, KVS, DSSSB, etc.

7. Police Recruitment Exams: SI, Constable, ASI, etc.

8. Other Government Exams: LIC AAO, DRDO, ISRO, FCI, ESIC, etc.

How to Download Admit Card?

Our website provides admit cards for various government recruitments, including:

1. Visit India Sarkari Naukri website

2. Click on the "Latest Admit Card" section.

3. Find and click on your exam name.

4. Log in using your Registration Number and DOB.

5. Click the "Download Admit Card" button.

6. Save the admit card in PDF format and take a printout.

How to Download Admit Card?

Carefully check all the details before downloading the admit card.

Carry a hard copy of the admit card along with a valid ID proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving License) to the exam center.

Arrive at the exam center well in advance of the designated reporting time.

Read and follow the exam-related instructions carefully.
